2Moons the real time browser based space strategy game

2Moons is a space strategy game with players playing together at the same time trying to be the best. A Standard Browser is all that's needed to the play.

2Moons will always be free to play, there are no purchase resource type options
It is hosted by the author of Healbot, on the same site as the Healbot website and the main administrator of 2Moons is the author of Healbot
